"We will engage in honest, fair, and ethical behavior with integrity at the heart of every decision and action."

Honest, sustainable progress never comes at the loss of another person. I always strive to be open and transparent about my actions as an RA, even the times I feel I have fallen short. We all must be open to ask questions, even if it is about our previous actions. This role requires fairness as well as there are many people with stakes in each decision. The Norse Life Community must also feel a place of belonging to voice these opinions as well, so it is vital we create an ethical form of communication that does not put down or take lightly the opinions of others.
